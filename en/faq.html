FAQ

How does Machbarschaft work for you as a helper?

On the map of our free app "Machbarschaft" you can find out who needs help in your area.
If you click on the request, you will find details about the type of request. Once you have decided to accept the request, the phone number of the person seeking help will be displayed. Call the person and clarify the exact need, time frame and payment method. Let us know when you are on the road and we will automatically inform the person seeking help.
Let's go - We look forward to your feedback!

How does the transfer of goods and payment work?

In general, this is a free service based on your voluntary commitment. Please discuss the shopping list in advance by phone call with the person seeking your help. Further, you can also arrange the purchasing method individually (pay before or after the service? pay in cash or another method? In order to ensure full transparency, we ask you to keep all receipts. Please avoid physical contact when handing over and receiving the payment for the goods! Keep a minimum distance of 1m to 2m by parking your goods at the apartment door. The cash should optimally be placed in an envelope or similar in front of the door. Through this measures, both sides are protected from spreading the infection in the best possible way.
Here, you can read our detailed step by step behaviour guidelines

When is the settlement to be executed?

You determine the exact time frame by telephone with the person to be supported.

What do I do if I cannot reach the person by phone?

It is possible that the person cannot be reached by telephone at short notice. Here we ask you to try again. If it does not work again, you are welcome to reject the request, so that it will be added to the request pool again.

Target audience/ persona:

"Machbarschaft" was brought to life for a specific reason - the Corona crisis. We want to connect people with each other, emphasize solidarity and above all provide help where it is urgently needed.
The offer of “Machbarschaft” is directed at people who are subject to greater restrictions than the general public due to COVID-19. Thus, they rely on extensive support through social contacts who e.g. can conduct everyday tasks outdoor such as grocery shopping or get medicine at the pharmacy. Above all, we especially support groups at high risk including elderly as well as people with previous illnesses.
We want to connect people who belong to a risk group due to age or health reasons with people who voluntarily support them during this time and thus contribute to their protection.

Our guidelines are based on the following values:

Protection – Our primary goal is to slow the spread of the COVID-19 virus and to protect risk groups. When handing over the shopping, we aks you to keep at least 1 - 2 m distance by placing the shopping in front of the apartment door. In case of cash payment, the appropriate cash is placed in an envelope in front of the apartment door.
Commitment – Agreed transactions are carried out to the best of our knowledge and belief and within the agreed time frame.
Trust – In order to protect those seeking help in the best possible way, we carry out an individual identity check. In addition, keep the invoice document of the purchases made for transparency maintenance.
Discretion – We handle your personal data responsibly and protect the privacy of helpers and people seeking help.
Voluntary & neighbourly commitment – No remuneration is claimed for the service provided. We ask for an attentive and careful contact between each other. Especially in this exceptional situation, our society depends on everyone sticking together as best as they can.
