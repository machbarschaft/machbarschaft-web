MACHBARSCHAFT

Machbarschaft connects elderly people without access to the internet in a network of neighbours

learn more!

Turn neighborhoods into MACHBARSCHAFTEN with us.

You want to see for yourself what it sounds like when you call our service? Just call +49 40 299960980 and find out! Your data will be transferred to our database and your order already arrives in our app. If your order is not accepted, you will receive a phone call after 24 hours that your order could not be processed yet, but you can extend the order. You can test both sequences by choosing between both at the beginning of the call (so you don't have to wait 24 hours for your call back).

Elderly people are currently particularly vulnerable and in need of help, but often have no access to digital neighbourhood assistance.

A call to machbarschaft already helps.

Elderly needy neighbours call the machbarschaft telephone number

A bot records the request - whether purchasing or another transaction

Our machbarschaft app shows the requests in the neighbourhood

Recommendations and flyer for people seeking help

Our help seekers make a simple phone call via our free hotline* and your requests are processed via artificial intelligence systems and finally displayed to our MACHBAR:IN as an order on the app. Without any personal contact! Download recommendation!
To ensure that you as MACHBAR:IN and helper:in are protected against infection in the best possible way, we have already developed detailed rules of conduct for you and our helpers that correspond to the RKI guidelines. So everybody is protected! Download flyer!

Meet our family

We at MACHBARSCHAFT are a team of 21 people with different professional backgrounds and widely scattered residences throughout Germany. What connects us? We were brought together by the common vision of not leaving anyone in (self-)quarantine during this COVID-19 crisis due to lack of internet access in a drastic supply gap.
